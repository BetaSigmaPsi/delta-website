Beta Sigma Psi University of Nebraska-Lincoln

Spritual

We are strongly tied to the Lutheran tradition of faith. We attend worship at both the Lutheran Student Center and the Lutheran Chapel regularly. The group also leads weekly bible studies each semester.

Scholastic

The reason we are all at college in the first place is for our education. Thus, strong scholastic achievement by each member is important. This is fostered by frequent study hours and guidance from upper-classmen.

Social

All work and no play makes Marty a dull boy, so we also make sure to have a healthy amount of fun. This ranges from competing in intramural sports to casual get-togethers to semesterly formal events and dances.

About Beta Sig

Beta Sigma Psi is a National Lutheran Fraternity, founded in 1925. Our goal is to provide a positive environment where Lutheran men can grow Spiritually, Scholastically, and Socially. We are not just another fraternity. Delta Chapter was founded in 1929 and is located at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln.

Our Mission Statement: To promote an organization of communicant Lutheran men who recognize that life rooted with Christ in God is the only true goal of human existence and who therefore foster, encourage, and inspire activities and relationships which promote this end.
